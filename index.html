Project Team 13: Visualizing News during the COVID-19 Pandemic, DS4200 F20

Edwin Shen, Iryna Soloviova, Naman Dugar

Service-Learning Course Project as part of DS 4200 F20: Information Visualization, taught by Prof. Cody Dunne, Data Visualization @ Khoury, Northeastern University.

Abstract

As the world becomes more interconnected, accurate and correct news and information becomes highly important. However, as there are dozens of journalism sites and websites one can use, a reader may prefer sources that have particular biases or use certain words. In this project, we will analyze the language in various news sources during the COVID-19 pandemic and extract keywords to visualize the types of news media bias each particular source has.

Keywords: Media bias, fake news, linguistic analysis.
